Device Management

Device Management is an integral part of the District Without Limits package. As more iPads, Chromebooks, etc. are provided to your students and staff, the tracking and management of these devices is critical. This application was developed with direct feedback from CIOs across the country.

Introduction
  • Real-time tracking and geo-location capability of all devices
  • Simple and convenient mobile application (iOS, Android, Microsoft) available in app store for download
  • Powerful authority control to assign device managers to view only specific devices they are managing
  • Collaboration view allows device managers and users to communicate through message board, email/text message, and provides historical log of all information (tracking repairs, shipping/returns, etc.) for audit/management purposes.
  • Dynamic reporting allows district to track metrics and important data related to devices, including:
    • Recycle/Refresh periods for devices
    • Number of devices at each location
    • % of devices at the organization
    • # of non-returned devices
