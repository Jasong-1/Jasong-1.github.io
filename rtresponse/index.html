MISSION

Make Process Automation simple and affordable on the Internet.

OUR SOLUTION

Right Response LLC is a company that provides the Process Automation -Platform as a Service (PA-PaaS) on the Internet. Our technology can map, automate, and connect any process needed by individuals or companies at an affordable cost. With years of experience in process automation for different industries,

the company has developed many ready-to-use process templates to:

  • Automate time-consuming manual processes to increase efficiency

  • Serve as middleware to connect information islands that exist within a company or across the Internet

  • Track and report real-time transactional information to minimize the information gap delayed by the processing time

  • Map out knowledge and "know-how" to protect, maintain, and evolve Intellectual Property

  • Implement "Smart-Contract" to assure transaction safety, reliability, and credibility

  • Apply AI self-learning algorithms to reduce operational mistakes and increase efficiency

  • Adaptively adjust process flows by following managing rules and real-time Key Performance Indices (KPI) to assist decision-making

  • Enable staff to Work-From-Home (WFH)

  • Help individuals collaborate in their daily lives on the Internet

The automated process templates are browser-based that support all desktop and mobile devices and are also configurable for specific needs. Our platform solution is packaged into the Office Without Limits product line for any company looking for process automation to increase operational efficiency and save costs. Right Response also uses the same platform to launch the Collaborative Network (CN). CN can enable individuals to use Process Automation technology to collaborate with other people on the Internet in their daily life to achieve common goals.
